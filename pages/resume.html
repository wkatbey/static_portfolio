Active Solutions Group: Voice Platform

As a Full-Stack Developer at Active Solutions Group, I developed the Voice Platform, a web application, whose purpose is to automate the new-client process. There are two interfaces to the application: Client-Side, and Staff-Side.

Typically, a client would submit a ticket, asking for a specific set of business phones, with certain add-ons. To create the order, staff login to the Voice Platform, input the quantity of phones, the phone types, and a unique pin. The application then generates a quote.

Following this, the client uses the unique pin to login to the Client-Side Form, whereby they can input their locations, configure their extensions (based on how many phones they requested), and port and/or create phone numbers.

Once the client completes this form, staff can review their submission and verify it.
